Rock & Roll, jazz and rhythm & blues guitar weekends, workshops and on-stage performances in the yorkshire countryside

Get away from everyday life to enjoy playing your guitar along with other like-minded people “ I’ve played more in the last two days than I have in the past ten years” (Kevin Harvey)

Play along with Angelo Palladino, one of the best guitar/vocalist in the country and also an inspirational teacher. Angelo has supported Sting at Maddison Square Gardens, The Chieftans at The Royal Albert Hall, as well as many other major artists(see Tutors).

Learn new pieces or look to re-arrange old favourites along with other players of similar abilities. “I’ve learned more in the last five minutes than I have in the previous two years” (Roger Wainwright).

Rekindle your enthusiasm for playing. “This has really fired my enthusiasm to play again. What a great time! (Dave Sumpner).

You will learn how to play a range of pieces and also have the opportunity to perform in front of an audience, playing along with professionals. “It’s taken me four days to come down to earth. What a fantastic experience” (Jem Smith).

Build up your confidence to attempt pieces that you never felt able to attempt previously. “Had a superb time, thoroughly enjoyed it and would be keen to join in again. In the meantime if you know anybody else looking for a new band member, please let me know. ( Carl Chambers).

Enjoy lovely food and drink in comfortable, friendly surroundings. “What a great weekend; one of the best. Great company, great location, great food, great tuition and most of all, just great fun”. (Peter Dickinson).

You'll learn how to play a range of pieces and you will have the opportunity to play as part of a group, play individually, provide backing for others and sing, if you want to!
